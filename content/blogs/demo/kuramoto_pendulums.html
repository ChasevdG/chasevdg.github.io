Kuramoto pendulums

Huygens' clocks, Kuramoto style: N pendulums hang from a shared beam, and each phase follows θ̇ᵢ = ωᵢ + K r sin(ψ − θᵢ). The beam sways with the mean field r eiψ, so it sits still while the pendulums disagree and rocks visibly once they lock. Raise K to watch them fall into step; the inset circle shows the phases and the mean-field arrow.
